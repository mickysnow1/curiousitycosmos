Frequently Asked Questions

Is LearnSpark suitable for my 3-year-old?

Absolutely! Our toddler program (ages 1-3) focuses on sensory exploration, basic colors, shapes, and simple cause-and-effect activities. All content is developmentally appropriate and designed to spark natural curiosity.

How do you balance screen time with offline activities?

We follow a 50/50 approach - for every digital activity, there's a corresponding offline activity. For example, if your child learns about animals on-screen, they'll get a nature scavenger hunt to find animal homes in your backyard.

Can teachers access my child's progress?

Yes, with your permission. Teachers can view learning progress, assign specific activities that align with classroom curriculum, and communicate directly with parents through our secure platform.

What if my child loses interest?

Our AI-powered system adapts to your child's interests and learning pace. If engagement drops, we automatically suggest new topics or activities. Plus, our learning consultants can help adjust the learning path.

Is my child's data safe and private?

Child privacy is our top priority. We're COPPA compliant, use end-to-end encryption, and never share personal data with third parties. Parents have full control over their child's data and can request deletion at any time.
